# MindfulWork App – Stress Free Offices

## Project Objective

To design an app prototype that helps employees manage workplace stress through personalized Smart Stress Relief Pod sessions using AI-driven recommendations, guided breathing exercises, mood-based lighting and aroma control, and reflective journaling.

---

## Design Thinking Process

### 1. Empathize & Define

- Conducted research and surveys with 10 participants across different job roles and age groups
- Created 10 detailed personas representing a range of employees
- Built 10 empathy maps capturing stress triggers, coping mechanisms, and relaxation preferences
- Identified key pain points: lack of private spaces, generic wellness programs, no on-demand relief

**Problem**
> How might we use AI-driven sensory modulation (lighting, sound, and aroma) to provide a discreet, instant wellness sanctuary that empowers employees to optimize their mental state during the workday?"

---

### 2. Ideate

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **MoodSync AI** | Automatically adjusts pod settings (music, lighting, aroma) based on stress inputs |
| **Breathe With Me** | Guided micro-meditation sessions with timer and breathing animation |
| **Pod Scheduler** | Employees book pod sessions to avoid crowding and plan ahead |
| **Post-Session Journal** | Users rate stress before and after sessions and record reflections |
| **Stress Tracking Dashboard** | Displays current stress level, session history, and 7-day progress |

---

### 3. Prototype

**Screens Designed:**
- Login / Welcome Screen
- Home Screen (Stress Dashboard + AI Suggestion + Upcoming Sessions)
- Sessions Screen (Booking, Upcoming, Past, Cancelled)
- Book Your Pod Screen (Calendar + Time Slot Picker)
- Pod Control Screen (MoodSync AI + Lighting, Aroma, Music Sliders)
- Breathe With Me Screen (Timer + Guided Breathing)
- Post-Session Journal Screen (Stress Sliders + Reflection + History)
- Profile Screen (Stats + Preferences + Notification Settings)
